# Royal Gold Theme Design Specification
## VOWLY AI — Premium Cinematic Wedding Experience

---

# Overview

Royal Gold is the flagship premium theme of VOWLY AI.

The design direction combines:
- Luxury editorial typography
- Cinematic black and gold atmosphere
- Royal heraldic ornaments
- Emotional storytelling
- Mobile-first immersive experience

The theme should feel like:
- A high-end wedding invitation
- A luxury perfume advertisement
- A handcrafted cinematic digital experience

Target emotional reaction:

> "Wow. This looks expensive."

# Performance Guidelines

Must be optimized for mobile Safari.

## Avoid
- Heavy backdrop blur
- Animated box-shadow
- Large filter animations
- Excessive particles

## Prefer
- SVG assets
- Opacity animation
- Transform animation
- Static overlays

---

# Design Principles

1. Less is more
2. Typography is the hero
3. Lighting creates depth
4. Motion enhances emotion
5. Every detail must feel premium
